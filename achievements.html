"Learn about the journey of Shireen Abu Akleh – the journalist who was never afraid to speak the truth."

  • Over 25 Years in Journalism – One of the most prominent Palestinian journalists, working with Al Jazeera since 1997.
  • Coverage of Major Events – Reported on key events like the Second Intifada, Israeli attacks on Gaza, and violations in Jerusalem and the West Bank.
  • A Trusted Voice in Arab Media – Became a household name, delivering accurate and fearless reports from the heart of the conflict.
  • Inspiring a Generation – Encouraged many young journalists, especially Palestinian women, to pursue journalism despite the risks.
  • Posthumous Recognition – Honored with multiple international awards for her bravery, including the Courage in Journalism Award.
  • Her Words Became Immortal – She once said:
    "I will not put down the camera, even if I become the story."
    Today, her story continues to inspire the fight for free press and justice.
